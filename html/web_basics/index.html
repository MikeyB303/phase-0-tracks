V A P O R W A V E

Vaporwave is the most aesthetic form of music known to mankind. It is mostly slowed down lounge or elevator music accompanied by edited VHS recordings.

Sometimes I'm standing at a party thinking "wow this song would sound a lot better slowed down and accompanied by weird visuals such as edited VHS recordings"

A few great examples of vaporwave songs are:

  • MACINTOSH PLUS - リサフランク420 / 現代のコンピュー
  • BLANK BANSHEE - Teen Pregnancy
  • HOME - Resonance

There are only two reasons to listen to vaporwave:

  1. Ironically
  2. Because you actually like it
